Ingredients:
 - 2 Indomie packs
 - 3 Seeds of fresh pepper
 - 1/2 Onion
 - 1 1/2 pack of Indomie seasoning
 - 1 cup of water

Directions:
 1. Slice onions and pepper into tiny bits.

 2. Heat water until it boils.
 
 3. Put the indomies, slice onions and pepper, indomie seasoning and allow to boil for 3 minutes.
 
 4. Dish and serve after it cools.
